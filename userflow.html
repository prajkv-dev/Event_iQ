Visitor Journey

  1. Register: Create profile and receive adaptive QR pass.
  2. Arrive: Scan QR at gate; system checks density and grants entry.
  3. Navigate: Use AR overlay to follow low‑congestion paths.
  4. Medic: Contact in case of a medical emergency.
  5. Exit: Receive staggered exit slot and guided route.
  6. Emergency: App switches to evacuation mode with nearest safe exits.
