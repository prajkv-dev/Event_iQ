Medical Delivery

Autonomous Emergency Medical Drops

Drones dispatched within seconds for first-aid kits, AEDs, medications, and blood units — integrated with crowd density data to navigate the fastest clear path.

🏥

Smart Payload Routing

AI calculates the optimal flight path using live crowd density data, wind speed, and obstacle maps to ensure fastest delivery.

⚡

Sub-90s Dispatch

From emergency trigger to airborne in under 90 seconds. Pre-loaded medical pods launch automatically on staff alert or panic button activation.

📦

Payload Types

AED defibrillators, naloxone kits, epinephrine auto-injectors, trauma bandages, blood units (cooled pods), and insulin.

🎯

Precision Landing

Vision-based landing on marked landing pads or direct hand-off to responders, with GPS ± 15 cm accuracy even indoors via UWB anchors.

🔁

Fleet Rotation

Hot-swap battery system ensures a drone is always available. Fleet of 6 drones per zone; two always on standby at full charge.

🔔

Real-time Notifications

Staff and medical teams receive live ETAs, payload contents, and landing zone updates via the SafeFlow Alerts system.

Aerial Survey

Camera Footage & Crowd Intelligence

Drones act as aerial crowd sensors — streaming live HD footage to the SafeFlow command center, detecting density hotspots, and triggering routing adjustments before congestion forms.

📷

4K Thermal + RGB Feed

Dual-sensor payload streams 4K RGB and thermal infrared simultaneously — detecting body heat clusters for crowd density mapping even at night.

🧠

AI Crowd Analytics

On-board edge inference counts people per zone, classifies movement patterns, and flags panic / stampede behavior in real time.

🗺️

Orthomosaic Mapping

Post-event photogrammetry stitches thousands of aerial frames into geo-referenced 3D maps for incident review and venue planning.

📡

Mesh Relay Network

Drones form a self-healing mesh relay, extending SafeFlow's 5G/Wi-Fi coverage to GPS-denied indoor zones like tunnels and underground halls.

🔒

Encrypted Streams

All footage is AES-256 encrypted in flight and stored on private on-premises servers — never sent to third-party clouds without admin approval.

⚙️

Autonomous Patrol Routes

Pre-programmed waypoint missions with dynamic re-routing triggered by crowd surge alerts from the SafeFlow density model.

Workflow

How It Works

Step 01
Incident Detected
Panic button, staff alert, or AI anomaly detection triggers an emergency event in the SafeFlow system with GPS coordinates.
Step 02
Drone Auto-Assigned
Fleet manager selects the nearest available drone, loads the appropriate medical payload pod, and calculates a crowd-aware flight path.
Step 03
Airborne < 90 Seconds
Drone lifts off automatically. Staff and medical teams receive a push notification with ETA, drone ID, and payload details.
Step 04
Live Feed to Command
On-board 4K camera streams to the SafeFlow command center in real time. AI annotates crowd density en route.
Step 05
Precision Delivery
Drone hovers at 2m, releases payload to responder or drops on marked landing pad. Confirmation photo logged automatically.
Step 06
Return & Recharge
Drone autonomously returns to the nearest docking station, recharges, and is cleared for the next mission — full cycle in under 12 minutes.
